# Gold Item Expansion

Gold Item Expansion adds a variety of golden-themed items and powerful potions, 
enhancing gameplay with more strategic and exciting options.

### Crafting Recipes
- **Compressed Gold Block**
- **Enchanted Golden Apple**
- **Golden Head**

### Brewing Recipes
- **Healing Potion (Instant Health III)**
- **Godly Healing Potion (Instant Health V)**
- **God Potion Series**

## God Potion Description

**God Potion**
- When used on players: grants level 5 of all positive buffs.
- When used on non-player entities: grants level 5 of all negative debuffs. (PvE)

**Undead God Potion**
- When used on undead entities: grants level 5 of all positive buffs.
- When used on non-undead entities: grants level 5 of all negative debuffs. (PvP)

**Arthropod God Potion**
- When used on arthropod entities: grants level 5 of all positive buffs.
- When used on non-arthropod entities: grants level 5 of all negative debuffs. (PvP)
